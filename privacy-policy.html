Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2025

Privacy at a Glance

  • We collect minimal personal data necessary to provide hair analysis services
  • Your hair photos are processed securely and not stored permanently
  • You have full control over your data with GDPR rights
  • We never sell your personal information to third parties
  • All data is encrypted and stored securely

1. Who We Are

Strandly ("we," "our," or "us") is a hair analysis service that provides personalized hair care recommendations. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website at strandly.com and our services.

Data Controller:
Strandly
Email: support@strandly.com

2. What Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Contact Information: Email address for service delivery
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe (we don't store credit card details)
  • Hair Photos: Images you upload for analysis
  • Quiz Responses: Your answers about hair type, concerns, and preferences
  • Location Data: Country/region for product availability (optional)

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our website and services
  • Analytics: Website performance and user behavior (anonymized)

3. How We Use Your Information

Primary Purposes (Contractual Basis)

  • Analyze your hair photos and quiz responses
  • Generate personalized hair care recommendations
  • Deliver analysis results via email
  • Process payments for our $29 service
  • Provide customer support

Secondary Purposes (Legitimate Interest)

  • Improve our analysis algorithms and recommendations
  • Enhance website functionality and user experience
  • Analyze service performance and usage patterns
  • Prevent fraud and ensure service security

With Your Consent

  • Send follow-up emails with hair care tips (opt-out anytime)
  • Marketing communications (opt-in required)
  • Share testimonials or success stories (with explicit permission)

4. Data Storage and Security

Storage Locations

Your data is stored securely on servers located in the United States and European Union, with appropriate safeguards for international transfers.

Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure cloud storage with enterprise-grade encryption
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Limited access controls and employee training
  • PCI DSS compliance for payment processing

Data Retention

  • Hair Photos: Processed immediately and deleted within 30 days
  • Quiz Data: Retained for 2 years to improve recommendations
  • Email Address: Retained until you request deletion or unsubscribe
  • Payment Records: Retained for 7 years for legal/tax requirements
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized and retained for 3 years

5. Your GDPR Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

Right of Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you

Right to Rectification

Correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")

Right to Restrict Processing

Limit how we use your personal data

Right to Data Portability

Receive your data in a machine-readable format

Right to Object

Object to processing based on legitimate interests

How to Exercise Your Rights:
Email us at privacy@strandly.com or support@strandly.com
We will respond within 30 days of your request.

Website Hosting

Analytics (If Applicable)

We may use privacy-focused analytics tools that don't use cookies or track personal information.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and tracking:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality
  • Payment Cookies: Necessary for Stripe payment processing
  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

8. International Users

Our service is available globally. If you're outside the United States:

  • Your data may be transferred to the US for processing
  • We ensure adequate protections for international transfers
  • EU users have full GDPR rights regardless of data location
  • We comply with local data protection laws where applicable

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

  • Updated "Last modified" date at the top of this policy
