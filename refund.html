3. EU Consumer Rights (GDPR Compliance)

14-Day Cooling-Off Period:

For customers in the European Union, you have enhanced consumer protection rights under GDPR:

  • 14-Day Right of Withdrawal: Cancel your purchase within 14 days for any reason
  • No Questions Asked: Full refund without providing justification
  • Immediate Processing: Refunds initiated within 24 hours of valid request
  • Complete Data Deletion: Full removal of personal data upon refund request
  • GDPR Data Rights: Automatic exercise of your right to erasure
  • Consent Withdrawal: Refund constitutes withdrawal of data processing consent

GDPR Data Protection Rights During Refunds:

When requesting a refund, you automatically exercise several GDPR rights:

  • Right to Erasure (Article 17): Your personal data is deleted within 24 hours
  • Right to Withdraw Consent (Article 7): Processing consent is immediately withdrawn
  • Right to Portability (Article 20): Data export available before deletion
  • Right to Object (Article 21): Object to any further processing

Personal Data Handling During Refunds:

Our GDPR-compliant refund process ensures:

  • Immediate Data Processing Halt: All data processing stops upon refund request
  • Automated Data Deletion: Personal data removed from all systems
  • Third-Party Notification: Data processors notified of deletion requirement
  • Proof of Deletion: Confirmation provided upon request

Digital Content Rights:

Since our service involves digital content (hair analysis report), the cooling-off period begins when:

  • Payment is successfully processed
  • You receive confirmation of your purchase
  • You have not yet started the hair analysis process
